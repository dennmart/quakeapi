Simple Earthquake API

Just a simple earthquake API, try it out!

Get all earthquakes

/earthquakes.json

Get all earthquakes from a single date from a timestamp (Example: '1368582600' => May 15, 2013)

/earthquakes.json?on=1368582600

Get all earthquakes since a specific time from a timestamp (Example: '1368582600' => all since May 15, 2013 at 1:50:00 AM UTC)

/earthquakes.json?since=1368582600

Get all earthquakes over a specific magnitude

/earthquakes.json?over=4.5

Get all earthquakes within a five mile radius of a latitude and longitude (Example: Latitude 35.40, Longitude -120.70)

/earthquakes.json?near=35.40,-120.70

You can limit the number of earthquakes returned by passing a limit

/earthquakes.json?limit=10

You can also combine multiple parameters

/earthquakes.json?over=3.0&near=32.67,-121.50
